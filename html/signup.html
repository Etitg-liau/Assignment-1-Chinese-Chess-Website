Still unsure of your decision? Here's some more benefits:

We have outings during holidays for members to keep in touch and have fun, may even take part in important events,
like the banquet (picture above) , also attended by Singapore's number 1 Chinese Chess Player.

There will be events where we join with other Chinese Chess clubs from other schools to interact and learn together.

Occasionally we will have a mini-tournament among each division and prizes will be awarded to the top 3 of each division. This ensures members get to understand the competition atmosphere so that they will not be clueless about what to do when going for external competitions.
